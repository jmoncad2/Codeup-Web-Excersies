Big Bend: Comes with its own natural Jacuzzi, known as Langford Hot Springs, that stays at about 105 degree Fahrenheit.
Big Thicket: Spans 113,121.96 acres of land and water spread over 7 counties in southeast Texas

Saint Croix: St. Croix is known for its Smallmouth bass fishing.
Castle Mountains: Has a Mine Area, an open pit gold mine in the southern Castle Mountains owned by Canadian NewCastle Gold Ltd.
Channel Islands: In spring 2006 Santa Cruz Island(One of the Channel Islands) was home to the first bald eagle chick to hatch unaided by humans on the Channel Islands in over 50 years.
Fort Foote: Fort Foote Park is part of the Civil War Defenses of Washington
Fort McHenry: For 200 years Fort McHenry has guarded the American flag in war and peace.
